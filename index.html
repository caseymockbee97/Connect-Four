↓Questions you might have↓

How do I win Connect Four?

  Line up four of your game pieces in a row vertically, horizontally, or diagonally before your opponent.

How do I play this version of Connect Four?

  When your game piece is visible under "Who's turn is it?", click the slot you want to drop your game piece into and your piece will be dropped to the bottom.

How can I take advantage of the score counter you so thoughtfully put in the game?

  If you want to keep playing after each win you can select rematch and the game will keep score for you. If you are facing a new opponent then select reset score to start with a fresh score board. The player who starts will alternate each round.
